SWHub is a full Go/Baduk/Weiqi study and play app. Train offline, analyse with KataGo, work with SGFs and your own game collection, search positions, join teaching rooms, and keep your statistics in sync across devices.

Its strongest areas are **KataGo analysis**, **tsumego training**, and **pattern search**—three connected tools for understanding positions, testing your reading, and finding recurring shapes in real games.

## Download SWHub

[Download the latest release on GitHub](https://github.com/SoumyaK4/WeiqiHub/releases).

- **Android:** choose the **Universal** build if you are unsure which APK matches your device.
- **Desktop:** download the release asset for your platform.
- **macOS:** the current build is experimental, so some features may not work as expected.

## What You Can Do

### Study your SGF collection

Import individual SGFs or complete folders in SGF Management. Keep a personal library, remove duplicates during import, and explore games in context.

### Search positions and openings

Use Pattern Search to investigate games through **Fuseki**, **Joseki**, or a selected board area. Narrow results with metadata filters, save useful snapshots and search history with pattern thumbnails, then open a game directly from the results. The personal ~250,000-pro-game database is available to coffee supporters.

### Explore joseki

Browse the new Joseki page, which includes approximately 5,000 variations for focused opening study.

### Train consistently

Work through curated collections and custom tasks, including **Count** and **Status** tsumego. SRS schedules reviews, while the session timer and ETA help you pace a study session. Auto-advance and mistake-management settings let you tailor the practice flow.

### Analyse games with KataGo

Use a local KataGo engine or connect to a remote WebSocket engine. Analysis and play modes include score, win-rate and point-loss graphs, ownership, rank and top-move overlays, plus one-click tsumego creation from a game. An experimental Career Mode lets you play against Human KataGo styles at selected ranks.

### Review, play and teach together

Use visual game-tree navigation, variations, comments, board markups, score estimation, SGF load/save, and opening positions in KataGo. Teaching rooms support password-protected sessions, voice broadcasting, chat, and handing Black or White to a student from any position. Teaching rooms are currently available to coffee supporters and teachers.

### Keep your progress with you

SWHub improves statistics synchronisation between devices, so your study history travels with you.
